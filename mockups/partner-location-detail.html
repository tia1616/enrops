5. Data model

New table: partner_locations

  • partner_name · location_name
  • street_address · city · state · zip
  • google_place_id · lat · lng · address_verified_at
  • main_phone (the one instructors see)
  • arrival_policy (text) · dismissal_policy (text)
  • extracted_from_source (text — what Dora pulled from; audit field)
  • extracted_at · extraction_confidence (jsonb per-field)

New table: partner_location_contacts

  • partner_location_id (FK)
  • name · role · phone · email
  • is_primary (boolean — instructor-facing one)
  • order_index

Linkage

Existing program_locations table gets a new FK column partner_location_id → partner_locations.id. The text district column you cleaned up 2026-05-21 stays for now; we can fold it into partner_locations.partner_name in a later pass.
